Who We Are

About Patel Samaj

The Patel Samaj of Indore District is a vibrant community united by shared heritage, values, and a common goal of social upliftment. With deep roots in agriculture, trade, and education, we are committed to the welfare of every member.

"समाज की शक्ति एकता में है, एकता की शक्ति संगठन में है।"
— Community Motto

Rich History

Tracing our roots back centuries, the Patel community has been the backbone of agriculture and trade in Malwa region, Madhya Pradesh.

Our Mission

To empower every community member with access to information, resources, and opportunities that enhance their quality of life.

Social Activities

Organizing blood donation camps, scholarship drives, skill development programs, and cultural festivals throughout the year.

Our Vision

A digitally connected, educationally advanced, and economically prosperous Patel community across all villages of Indore District.
